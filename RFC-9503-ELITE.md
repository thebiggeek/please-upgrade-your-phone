## Abstract

This document defines the Enhanced Load Intelligence & Traffic Engineering
framework (ELITE), a standardized TELCO operational methodology where network
congestion is not solved, but instead redistributed, renamed, rebranded,
marketed, segmented, optimized, prioritized, and monetized.

ELITE ensures that:

* premium users receive premium explanations
* ordinary users receive ordinary congestion
* reliability becomes a subscription feature
* traffic engineering becomes customer differentiation
* marketing budgets exceed RF planning budgets
