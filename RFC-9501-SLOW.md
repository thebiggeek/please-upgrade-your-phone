## Abstract

This document defines the Seamless Latency & Optimized Wirelessness
framework (SLOW), a standardized TELCO operational model where poor indoor
coverage, weak basement connectivity, mobility-related signal degradation,
and unusable handoff behavior are reclassified as subscriber movement issues.

SLOW additionally introduces advanced slice-based blame redistribution
techniques ensuring that coverage limitations are attributed to:

* mobility patterns
* structural interference
* environmental conditions
* dynamic slice allocation
* subscriber positioning
* unsupported handset optimization

rather than network infrastructure.
